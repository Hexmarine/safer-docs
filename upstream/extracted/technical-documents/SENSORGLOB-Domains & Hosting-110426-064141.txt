Sendgrid

SendGrid’s US-based data centers are located in Herndon, VA; Las Vegas, NV; and Chicago, IL.
When you send mail, it will go to the inbound data center that is closest to you in order to
transfer mail to our pipeline quickly. Mail is not processed at these locations, but is forwarded to
our US-based data centers for processing and sending.
https://sendgrid.com/files/SendGrid-FAQ.pdf
