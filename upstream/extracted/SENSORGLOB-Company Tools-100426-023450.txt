Tools and Platforms We Utilize for Seamless Collaboration and Innovation
Welcome to our comprehensive overview of the tools and platforms we leverage to streamline
communication, enhance design processes, maintain meticulous documentation, and power our
cutting-edge technology. Below, you'll find an organized breakdown of our toolkit, ensuring a
seamless workflow across all departments. For any access or account-related inquiries, don't

Communication
Facilitating effective communication is pivotal to our success. We employ a variety of tools to
keep everyone connected and informed:
1. Teams: Conduct video and audio calls while enabling real-time chat functionality.
2. Outlook 365: Manage emails and calendars with ease, ensuring timely correspondence and
  scheduling.


Design
The design phase is where innovation comes to life. Our creative endeavours are powered by:
1. Miro: Empowering diagramming activities, aiding in visualizing complex concepts.
2. Figma: Fueling our UX design processes, enabling collaborative and intuitive user
   experiences.
3. Adobe InDesign: For impeccable page layout and polished design aesthetics.


Documentation
Thorough documentation is the cornerstone of our knowledge-sharing culture. We rely on:
1. Office 365: Harnessing Word, Excel, and PowerPoint for well-structured documentation.
2. Confluence: Serving as a repository for user stories and comprehensive company-wide
   product documentation.
3. Jira: Efficiently managing bug tracking and road mapping, ensuring seamless project
   progress.
4. 1Password: Ensuring robust password management and data security.
5. OneDrive: Securely storing and sharing essential documents.


Technology
Our technological prowess is supported by a collection of powerful platforms:
1. Sendgrid: Orchestrating email communication from our products, ensuring efficient
   correspondence.
2. Twilio: Managing SIM cards, a fundamental component of our communication infrastructure.
3. CodeTwo: Crafting consistent and professional email signatures.
4. AWS (Amazon Web Services): Hosting our platforms, guaranteeing scalability and reliability.
5. Google Play: Serving as the hub for hosting our Android applications.
6. Apple App Store Connect: Facilitating the deployment and management of our iOS apps.
7. Google Cloud: Enhancing our platform with supplementary API hosting.
8. Tricentis Testim: Enabling automated testing to ensure the integrity and quality of our
   products.
